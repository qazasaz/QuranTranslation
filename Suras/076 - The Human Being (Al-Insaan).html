76:0 In the Name of GOD, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.
 
76:1 Is it not a fact that there was a long time when the human being was nothing to be mentioned?
 
76:2 We created the human being from a fertilized single cell, in order to test him. Thus, we made him a hearer and a seer.
 
76:3 We showed him the path; he is either appreciative, or unappreciative.
 
76:4 We prepared for the disbelievers chains, beam rings, and a blazing Hell.
 
76:5 As for the virtuous, they will drink from cups spiced with nectar.
 
76:6 A spring that is reserved for GOD's servants; it will gush out as they will.
 
76:7 They fulfill their pledges, and reverence a day that its ugliness is wide spread.
 
76:8 They donate their favorite food to the poor, the orphan, and the captive.
 
76:9 "We feed you for the sake of GOD; we expect no reward from you, nor thanks.
 
76:10 "We fear from our Lord a day that is full of misery and trouble."
 
76:11 Therefore, GOD protects them from the evils of that day, and gives them joy and contentment.
 
76:12 He rewards them for their steadfastness with Paradise, and silk.
 
76:13 They relax therein on luxurious thrones. They suffer neither the heat of the sun, nor any cold.
 
76:14 The shade covers them therein, and the fruits are brought within reach.
 
76:15 They are served with silver containers and cups that are translucent.
 
76:16 Translucent cups, made of silver; they are designed for a specific amount.
 
76:17 They enjoy drinks of delicious flavors in cups therein.
 
76:18 From a spring therein known as Salsabeel.
 
76:19 Serving them will be immortal servants. When you see them, they will look like scattered pearls.
 
76:20 Wherever you look, you will see bliss, and a wonderful dominion.
 
76:21 On them will be clothes of green velvet, satin, and silver ornaments. Their Lord will provide them with pure drinks.
 
76:22 This is the reward that awaits you, for your efforts have been appreciated.
 
76:23 We have revealed to you the Quran with a special revelation from us.
 
76:24 You shall be patient for your Lord's judgment, and do not obey any sinner or any disbeliever among them.
 
76:25 And commemorate the name of your Lord day and night.
 
76:26 In the night, fall prostrate before Him, and meditate on Him many a long night.
 
76:27 These people are preoccupied with this fleeting one, while disregarding - just ahead of them - a heavy day.
 
76:28 We created them, and organized their settlement, and, whenever we will, we can substitute similar ones in their place.
 
76:29 This is a reminder; whoever wills shall choose the path to his Lord.
 
76:30 Unless GOD wills, nothing happens even if you will. GOD is the best Knower, Wise.
 
76:31 He admits whomever He wills into His mercy. As for the transgressors, He has prepared for them a painful retribution.
