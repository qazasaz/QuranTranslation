81:0 In the Name of GOD, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.
 
81:1 When the sun is rolled,
 
81:2 When the stars are fell and crashed into each other,
 
81:3 When the mountains are moved and wiped out,
 
81:4 When the reproduction is halted,
 
81:5 When the beasts are summoned,
 
81:6 When the oceans are set aflame,
 
81:7 When the souls are restored to their bodies,
 
81:8 When the girl who was buried alive is asked,
 
81:9 For what crime she was killed,
 
81:10 When the screens are opened,
 
81:11 When the heaven is removed,
 
81:12 When the Hell is ignited,
 
81:13 When the Paradise is presented,
 
81:14 Every soul will know everything it brought.
 
81:15 I solemnly swear by the collapsed ones,
 
81:16 By the ones running in their orbits,
 
81:17 By the night as it falls,
 
81:18 By the morn as it breathes,
 
81:19 This is the utterance of an honorable Messenger.
 
81:20 Authorized by the Possessor of the Throne, fully supported.
 
81:21 He shall be obeyed and trusted.
 
81:22 Your friend is not crazy.
 
81:23 He indeed saw him at the bright horizon.
 
81:24 He is not keeping unknown secret.
 
81:25 It is not the utterance of a rejected devil.
 
81:26 Now then, where will you go?
 
81:27 This is a Message to the world.
 
81:28 For those who wish to go straight.
 
81:29 Your will absolutely depends on the will of GOD, Lord of the Universe.
