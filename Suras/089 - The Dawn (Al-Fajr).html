89:0 In the Name of GOD, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.
 
89:1 By the dawn,
 
89:2 By the ten nights,
 
89:3 By the even and the odd,
 
89:4 By the night as it passes,
 
89:5 It is a profound oath for one who possesses intelligence, isn't it?
 
89:6 Have you noted what your Lord did to `Ad?
 
89:7 Erum; the one with tall buildings.
 
89:8 None like it was built among the cities.
 
89:9 Also Thamud, who carved the rocks in their valley.
 
89:10 And Pharaoh who possessed might.
 
89:11 They all transgressed in their land.
 
89:12 They spread evil throughout.
 
89:13 At the end, your Lord poured upon them a whipping retribution.
 
89:14 Your Lord is always watching.
 
89:15 When the human being is tested by his Lord, through blessings and joy, he says, "My Lord is generous towards me."
 
89:16 But if He tests him through reduction in provisions, he says, "My Lord is humiliating me!"
 
89:17 No! It is you who do not regard the orphan.
 
89:18 And not advocate charity towards the poor.
 
89:19 And consume the inheritance (of orphans) selfishly.
 
89:20 And love the money intensely.
 
89:21 However, when the earth is crushed and scattered to pieces,
 
89:22 When your Lord comes, together with the Angels in row after row,
 
89:23 On that day, Gehenna will be brought forth. On that day, the human being will remember, but that remembrance will be useless.
 
89:24 He will say, "Oh, I wish I prepared for my (eternal) life."
 
89:25 No retribution can be like His retribution of that day.
 
89:26 No confinement can be like His confinement.
 
89:27 O content soul,
 
89:28 Return to your Lord, pleased and pleasing.
 
89:29 Welcome into My servants.
 
89:30 Welcome into My Paradise.
