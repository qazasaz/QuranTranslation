75:0 In the Name of GOD, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.
 
75:1 I swear by the Resurrection Day.
 
75:2 And I swear by the blaming soul.
 
75:3 Does the human being think that we will not reconstruct his bones?
 
75:4 Yes, in fact we are able to reconstruct his finger tip.
 
75:5 But the human being regards only what he sees in front of him.
 
75:6 He steadily asks questions about the Resurrection Day!
 
75:7 Once the vision is sharpened.
 
75:8 the moon is eclipsed.
 
75:9 the sun and the moon crash into one another,
 
75:10 The human being will say on that day, "Where is the escape?"
 
75:11 Absolutely, there is no escape.
 
75:12 To your Lord, on that day, is the final destiny.
 
75:13 The human being will be informed, on that day, of everything he did to advance himself, and to regress himself.
 
75:14 The human being will be his own judge,
 
75:15 Even if he made his excuses.
 
75:16 Do not move your tongue to hasten it.
 
75:17 It is we who will collect it into Quran.
 
75:18 Once we recite it, you shall follow such a Quran.
 
75:19 Then it is we who will explain it.
 
75:20 In fact, you love this fleeting life.
 
75:21 You disregard the Hereafter.
 
75:22 Some faces, on that day, will be happy.
 
75:23 Looking at their Lord.
 
75:24 Other faces will be, on that day, miserable.
 
75:25 Realizing that they shall incur the most severe one.
 
75:26 In fact, when (the soul) reaches the throat.
 
75:27 And it is ordered; "Let go!",
 
75:28 He knows it is the end.
 
75:29 Each leg will lay motionless next to the other leg.
 
75:30 To your Lord, on that day, is the summoning.
 
75:31 For he neither confirmed, nor worshiped.
 
75:32 But he rejected and turned away.
 
75:33 Then he acted arrogantly with his family.
 
75:34 You have deserved this, you are worthy of it.
 
75:35 You are worthy of what you deserved.
 
75:36 Does the human being think that he will be left uncontrolled?
 
75:37 Was he not a cell fertilized by the ejected semen (sperm)?
 
75:38 Then he did become an embryo, then He recreated and designed it.
 
75:39 He made it into a pair; male and female.
 
75:40 Is He then unable to revive the dead?
