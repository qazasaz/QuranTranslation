95:0 In the Name of GOD, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.
 
95:1 By the fig and the olive,
 
95:2 By Sinai, Mount Sinai,
 
95:3 By this secured city,
 
95:4 Certainly, we created the human being in the best design.
 
95:5 Then placed him in the lowest of lowers.
 
95:6 Except those who believe and lead a righteous life; they receive a everlasting reward.
 
95:7 Why do you still reject the faith?
 
95:8 Is GOD not Most Wise, of all the wise ones?
