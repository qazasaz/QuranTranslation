51:0 In the Name of GOD, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.
 
51:1 By the blowing winds,
 
51:2 Bearing rain,
 
51:3 Bringing provisions,
 
51:4 Distributing them as commanded,
 
51:5 What is promised to you will surely come to pass.
 
51:6 The Judgment is inevitable.
 
51:7 By the sky in systematic order,
 
51:8 You continue to have different opinions.
 
51:9 Deviating therefrom are the deviators.
 
51:10 Damn the falsifiers!
 
51:11 In their blundering, they are totally heedless.
 
51:12 They question the Judgment Day.
 
51:13 The day they are presented to the fire.
 
51:14 Taste your punishment; this is what you used to challenge.
 
51:15 The righteous have deserved gardens and springs.
 
51:16 They receive what their Lord gives, for they used to be devoted one.
 
51:17 They did sleep short in the night.
 
51:18 At dawn, they prayed for forgiveness.
 
51:19 A share of their money was set aside for the beggar and the needy.
 
51:20 In the earth, there are signs for those who are certain,
 
51:21 And within yourselves; can't you see?
 
51:22 In the heaven is your provision, and what you are promised.
 
51:23 By the Lord of the heaven and the earth, this is as true as the fact that you can speak.
 
51:24 Have you noted the history of Abraham's honorable guests?
 
51:25 They visited him, saying, "Peace." He said, "Peace, strange people!"
 
51:26 He asked his family to prepare a fat calf.
 
51:27 When he offered it to them, he remarked, "Do you not eat?"
 
51:28 He harbored fear of them. They said, "Have no fear." and they gave good news of a knowledgeable son.
 
51:29 His wife was amazed, wrinkled her face and said; "I am a sterile old woman."
 
51:30 They said, "Thus said your Lord. He is Most Wise, Omniscient."
 
51:31 He said, "Any other mission, O Messengers?"
 
51:32 They said, "We have been dispatched to a guilty people.
 
51:33 "We will shower them with rocks of clay.
 
51:34 "Marked by your Lord for the transgressors."
 
51:35 We removed all the believers therefrom.
 
51:36 We did not find in it except one house of submitters.
 
51:37 We left there a proof for those who fear the painful retribution.
 
51:38 Also in Moses; we sent him to Pharaoh with profound authority.
 
51:39 But he turned away with his forces and said, "Magician, or crazy."
 
51:40 Therefore, we took him and his troops and threw them into the sea; he is the one to blame.
 
51:41 And in `Ad; we sent upon them disastrous wind.
 
51:42 Anything that it came upon was totally destroyed, it raised a cloud of dust.
 
51:43 And in Thamud; they were told, "Enjoy temporarily."
 
51:44 They rebelled against the command of their Lord. Therefore, the thunderbolt struck them as they looked.
 
51:45 They could never get up, nor were they helped.
 
51:46 And the people of Noah before that; they were wicked people.
 
51:47 We constructed the heaven with our hands, and we continue to expand it.
 
51:48 And we made the earth habitable; a perfect design.
 
51:49 We created a pair of everything, that you may take heed.
 
51:50 You shall run to GOD. I am a manifest warner to you from Him.
 
51:51 Do not set up beside GOD any other god. I am a manifest warner to you from Him.
 
51:52 Consistently, when a Messenger went to the previous generations, they said, "Magician." or "Crazy."
 
51:53 Did they make an agreement with each other? In fact, they are idolizing people.
 
51:54 You may disregard them; you can't be blamed.
 
51:55 And deliver Message, for the Message benefits the believers.
 
51:56 I did create the jinns and the humans just to worship Me.
 
51:57 I need no provisions from them, nor do I need them to feed Me.
 
51:58 GOD is the Provider, the Possessor of all power, the Supreme.
 
51:59 The transgressors have deserved a fate like the fate of their previous counterparts; therefore, they should not challenge.
 
51:60 Woe to those who disbelieved from the day that is awaiting them.
