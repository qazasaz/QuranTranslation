92:0 In the Name of GOD, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.
 
92:1 By the night as it covers,
 
92:2 By the day as it reveals,
 
92:3 By Him who created the male and the female,
 
92:4 Your priorities are of various kinds.
 
92:5 As for him who gives to charity and maintains righteousness,
 
92:6 And upholds the best,
 
92:7 We will direct him towards happiness.
 
92:8 But he who is stingy, although he is rich,
 
92:9 And rejects the best,
 
92:10 We will direct him towards suffering.
 
92:11 His money can't help him when he falls.
 
92:12 We provide the guidance.
 
92:13 We control the Hereafter, as well as this life.
 
92:14 I have warned you about the blazing Hellfire.
 
92:15 None enters there except the wicked,
 
92:16 Who rejects and turns away.
 
92:17 Avoiding it will be the righteous,
 
92:18 Who gives his money to be purified,
 
92:19 Seeking nothing in return for this charity.
 
92:20 Seeking only his Lord, Most High.
 
92:21 He will certainly attain salvation.
