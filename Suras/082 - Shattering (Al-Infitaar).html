82:0 In the Name of GOD, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.
 
82:1 When the heaven is shattered,
 
82:2 When the planets are scattered,
 
82:3 When the oceans are exploded,
 
82:4 When the graves are opened,
 
82:5 Every soul will find out what caused it to advance, and what caused it to regress.
 
82:6 O you human being, what diverted you from your Lord, Most Honorable?
 
82:7 The One who created you, designed you, and perfected you.
 
82:8 In whatever design He chose, He constructed it.
 
82:9 In fact, you reject the religion.
 
82:10 Be sure that there are watchers around you,
 
82:11 They are honest recorders,
 
82:12 They know everything you do.
 
82:13 Surely, the pious have deserved blessings.
 
82:14 While the wicked have deserved Hell.
 
82:15 They will incur it on the Judgment Day.
 
82:16 They never leave it.
 
82:17 Can you see what the Judgment Day is?
 
82:18 Can you comprehend what the Religious Day is?
 
82:19 That is the day when no soul can help another soul. All decisions, on that day, will belong to GOD.
