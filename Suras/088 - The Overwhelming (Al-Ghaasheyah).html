88:0 In the Name of GOD, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.
 
88:1 Did you get the news of the Overwhelming one?
 
88:2 Some faces on that day will be shamed.
 
88:3 Laboring and exhausted in vain.
 
88:4 Suffering in a blazing Hellfire.
 
88:5 Drinking from a flaming spring.
 
88:6 They will have bitter thorny food.
 
88:7 It never nourishes, nor satisfies hunger.
 
88:8 Other faces on that day will be full of joy.
 
88:9 Satisfied with their work.
 
88:10 In a High Paradise.
 
88:11 Wherein no nonsense is heard.
 
88:12 Therein a spring flows.
 
88:13 Wherein there are luxurious thrones on air.
 
88:14 The cups ready to be served.
 
88:15 The pitchers in rows.
 
88:16 The carpets spread out.
 
88:17 Why do they not reflect on the camels; how they are created?
 
88:18 The heaven; how it is raised?
 
88:19 The mountains; how they are set up?
 
88:20 The earth; how it is constructed?
 
88:21 You shall remind; just deliver this reminder.
 
88:22 You have no power over them.
 
88:23 Whoever turns away and disbelieves,
 
88:24 GOD will punish him with the greatest retribution.
 
88:25 To us is their final destiny.
 
88:26 Then we will call them to account.
